Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

1. Data Preservation

At TranslitMedia, we do not preserve your data. The majority of our tools run entirely "client-side," meaning the files you upload are processed directly within your web browser and never sent to our servers. Once you close the tab or refresh the page, all processed data is instantly lost from memory.

2. Automatic Deletion

In the rare event that a future tool requires server-side processing, any data reserved on our systems is automatically scheduled for permanent deletion within a few days (typically 24-48 hours). We maintain a strict policy of minimizing data retention to the absolute minimum required to perform the requested service.

3. Sensitive Data Warning

For your safety: Please do not upload highly sensitive, confidential, or personally identifiable information (PII) such as passwords, financial records, or government IDs.

4. Data Security & Liability

While we employ standard security measures to protect the integrity of our platform, TranslitMedia provides no guarantee against data leakage. By using our services, you acknowledge that no transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and you use these tools at your own risk. We are not liable for any loss or exposure of data resulting from the use of our services.
